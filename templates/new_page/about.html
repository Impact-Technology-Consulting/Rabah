Our Story

Rabah360 was launched in 2024 as a software company based in the Australia. Our team cares about helping churches and supporting their services through technology. We hope to improve the relationship between churches and members and are continuously working towards finding innovative tools that help servants perform their duties more effectively.

Today, Rabah360 is helping over ten thousand churches keep track of their members and guests...and so much more.

Our Mission

To EQUIP the Church, to build the body of Christ.

Our Value

At Rabah360, our Core Values can be summarized by the word
EQUIP.

E

Excellent Service
We're here to serve God's church and help simplify ministry
and administration by providing unrivaled support and
resources.

Q

Quality & Passion
We don't do half-way; mediocre is not in our vocabulary. We
are passionate about the local church. We work hard to
deliver quality and exceed expectations.

U

User Friendly
We aim to provide simple, effective solutions for ministry and
admin challenges. We seek to be intuitive, helpful, and
reliable.

I

Intentionally Affordable
We could charge more, but we don't need to. We want to be
affordable and accessible for ministries of all sizes in all
locations.

P

Partner in Ministry
Your mission is our mission. We're not just a software
company; we're a resource for ministry and building up the
body of Christ.
